## Tier 1: Strategic Partners

### 1. Safaricom (M-Pesa)

**Partner Profile:**
- Africa's largest mobile money platform
- 51M+ active users
- $314B annual transaction value
- Dominant in Kenya, expanding regionally

**Partnership Opportunity:**

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Type** | Technical + Marketing |
| **Objective** | Become the official M-Pesa MCP integration |
| **Value to Them** | Reduced support burden, developer adoption |
| **Value to Us** | Credibility, preferred status, API access |

**Proposed Collaboration:**
1. **Technical Partnership**
   - Early access to M-Pesa API updates
   - Direct engineering support channel
   - Co-developed SDK improvements
   - Sandbox environment for testing

2. **Marketing Partnership**
   - Featured in M-Pesa developer portal
   - Joint case studies
   - Co-branded documentation
   - Speaking opportunities at Safaricom events

3. **Commercial Terms**
   - No direct revenue share (open source)
   - Referral fee for enterprise leads: 5%
   - Marketing co-fund: $10,000/quarter

**Contact Strategy:**
- Entry point: M-Pesa Developer Relations team
- Decision maker: Head of Digital Payments
- Timeline: 3-6 months to close
- Proof points: Working integration, user testimonials
