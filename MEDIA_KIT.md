## üì∏ Photo/Video Guidelines

**Photography:**
- Real African developers in their natural environment
- Diverse representation across countries
- Modern, tech-forward aesthetics
- Warm, natural lighting

**Illustrations:**
- Clean, minimalist style
- Geometric patterns inspired by African textiles
- Isometric illustrations for technical concepts

**Color Treatment:**
- Vibrant but professional
- High contrast for accessibility
- Consistent with brand palette

### Video Guidelines

**Demo Videos:**
- 60-90 seconds maximum
- Show real code in real editors (VS Code, Cursor)
- Include voiceover or clear captions
- End with clear CTA

- 30-60 seconds
- Developer-focused
- Authentic, unscripted feel
